Why Does Ethical AI Use Matter?

AI tools like ChatGPT, Grammarly, or image generators can help with schoolwork, but using them without thinking can lead to plagiarism, misinformation, or overdependence. Ethical use means using these tools to help you learn — not to cheat or replace your thinking.

Examples of Ethical and Unethical Use

  • Ethical: Using AI to brainstorm ideas or improve grammar.
  • Unethical: Copying and pasting an AI-generated essay as your own.
  • Ethical: Asking AI to explain a difficult math concept.
  • Unethical: Using AI to answer test questions during an exam.

AI Tools and How to Use Them Right

Here are some tools you might use in school, and tips on how to use them responsibly:

  • ChatGPT: Great for asking questions, but cite if you use any full ideas or explanations.
  • Grammarly: Helps improve grammar, but don't let it change your entire writing style.
  • Image Generators: Use for creative projects, but don't claim AI images as your original artwork.
